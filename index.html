About the Directory

This is a compiled, research-verified guide to thrift stores, consignment shops, antique malls, and resale shops across Erie County PA, Crawford County PA, Venango County PA, and Western New York (Chautauqua, Cattaraugus, and Erie County NY). Every listing has been cross-referenced against current Facebook pages and Google listings. Hours change without notice. Stores close. New ones open. Always call ahead before making a trip — every listing marked "Call ahead to verify" means exactly that.

Before You Go

6 Rules for This Region

01

Always Call Ahead

Many stores run on limited or seasonal hours. A 60-second call saves a 45-minute drive. If this directory says "Call ahead to verify," treat that seriously.

02

Go Weekday Mornings

The best selection is gone by Saturday afternoon. Tuesday through Thursday before noon is when fresh inventory hits the floor and competition is lowest.

03

Goodwill & Salvation Army Restock Mid-Week

Most locations process donations and put out new inventory Tuesday through Thursday. Weekend shoppers are working through what's left.

04

Antique Malls Have Individual Vendor Hours

The building may be open but a specific booth may not be staffed. If you're driving for one vendor, call the mall first and ask about that dealer's schedule.

05

Estate Sales Move Fast

Arrive before opening on day one for the best picks. Return on the final day for steep discounts — many sales drop 50–75% to clear everything out.

06

Follow Stores on Facebook

Pop-up sales, surprise closures, new inventory drops — Facebook is how this region's thrift stores communicate in real time. The store's page is your best source of truth.
